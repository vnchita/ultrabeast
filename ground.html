GROUND

Ground types are one of the best types offensively. It is able to hit over a quarter of other types and many of its moves are commonly sought after for its power.

In Generations 1-3, all Ground type moves were categorized as Physical.

Attack pros & cons

Ground type moves are super effective against:

STEEL
FIRE
ELECTRIC
POISON
ROCK

Ground type moves are not very effective against:

BUG
GRASS

Ground type moves do not affect:

FLYING

Defense pros & cons

Ground type Pokèmon resist:

POISON
ROCK

Ground type Pokèmon are weak against:

ICE
GRASS
WATER

Ground type Pokèmon are immune to:

ELECTRIC

There are no recorded Ultrabeasts with this type.
