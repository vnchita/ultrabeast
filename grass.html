GRASS

The Grass type is one among three of the first types introduced to the player to easily explain the Pokèmon type concepts. It is one of the weakest types statistically because it is unable to hit many types for neutral or super effective damage, and it is also weak to a considerable amount of types.

In Generations 1-3, all Grass type moves were categorized as Special.

Grass type moves are super effective against:

Grass type moves are not very effective against:
