ROCK

Rock type is a relatively balanced type, with similar proportions in strengths and weaknesses. Most rock types have good physical defense, but in earlier generations, many rock types were also Ground type, giving it a quadrouple weakness to both grass and water.

In Generations 1-3, all Rock type moves were categorized as Physical.
