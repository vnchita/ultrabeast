PSYCHIC

In the first generation, Psychic types were immensely powerful because it virtually had no weaknesses. In the next generation, they rectified this with the introduction of Dark types and the increase in Bug type moves as well as fixing the Ghost type moves. After that, Psychic types were less dominant in the competitive scene.

In Generations 1-3, all Psychic type moves were categorized as Special.

Attack pros & cons

Psychic type moves are super effective against:

FIGHTING
POISON

Psychic type moves are not very effective against:

PSYCHIC
STEEL

Psychic type moves do not affect:

DARK
