DRAGON

Dragon types contain the most elusive and powerful Pokèmon. Most pseudo legendary Pokèmon, or Pokèmon with legendary-like stats, are Dragon type. As of right now--Generation 9--a whopping 19 legendaries are Dragon type.

In Generations 1-3, all Dragon type moves were categorized as Special.
