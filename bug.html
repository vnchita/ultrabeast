BUG

Many Bug types grow very quickly, and as a result they are mostly weak. It is also the worst type offensively.

In Generations 1-3, all Bug type moves were categorized as Physical.

Bug type moves are not very effective against:

POISON
FIRE
GHOST
FIGHTING
