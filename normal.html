NORMAL

Normal type Pokèmon are the most basic of all types. They are common in the early game.

In Generations 1-3, all Normal type moves were categorized as Physical.

Attack pros & cons

Normal type moves are not very effective against:

ROCK
STEEL

Normal type moves do not affect:

GHOST

Defense pros & cons

Normal type Pokèmon are weak against:

FIGHTING

There are no recorded Ultrabeasts with this type.
