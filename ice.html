ICE

As of Generation 9, Ice types are the rarest types, with only 60 Pokèmon in total. Most Ice Pokèmon have good defense stats, but they are not able to utilize this because of their poor resistances.

In Generations 1-3, all Ice type moves were categorized as Special.

Attack pros & cons

Ice type moves are super effective against:

DRAGON
GROUND
GRASS
FLYING

Ice type moves are not very effective against:

ICE
STEEL
WATER
FIRE

Defense pros & cons

Ice type Pokèmon resist:

ICE

Ice type Pokèmon are weak against:

FIRE
STEEL
ROCK
FIGHTING

There are no recorded Ultrabeasts with this type.
