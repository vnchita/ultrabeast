POISON

Poison types are considered as one of the weakest offensively. It's somewhat better defensively, but it is mostly utilized for status moves.

In Generations 1-3, all Poison type moves were categorized as Physical.

Attack pros & cons

Poison type moves are super effective against:

GRASS
FAIRY

Poison type moves are not very effective against:

GROUND
POISON
ROCK
GHOST

Poison type moves do not affect:

STEEL
