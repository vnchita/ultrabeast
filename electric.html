ELECTRIC

Electric types are quite good defensively, with only Ground type as its weakness. There are relatively few Electric types that exist.

Most Electric Pokèmon are based off of inanimate objects or rodents. A good example of this is the Pokèmon mascot Pikachu.

Attack pros & cons

Electric type moves are super effective against:

WATER
FLYING

Electric type moves are not very effective against:

ELECTRIC
DRAGON
GRASS

Electric type moves do not affect:

GROUND

Defense pros & cons

Electric type Pokèmon resist:

GRASS
FAIRY
STEEL

Electric type Pokèmon are weak against:

GROUND

Ultrabeasts with this type:

Xurkitree
